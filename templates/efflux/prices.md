# Prices

This is about the pricing of different packages

## Free

This is more info about the free trial

- Efflux CRM has a one month free trial.
- The free trial of Efflux includes all premium features.
- Corporate and small businesses are eligible for the free trial of Efflux.

## Premium

This is about the premium version

- Efflux CRM Premium costs $25 per month.
- The Premium version is recommended for small businesses.
- The Premium version costs $20 per month when you sign an annual contract.

## Professional

This is about the professional CRM version

- Efflux Professional is tailored to larger teams.
- Efflux Professional costs $70 per month.
- The annual cost of Efflux Professional is $60 a month.

## Corporate

This is about the pricing of corporate CRM version

- Efflux Corporate is designed for large enterprises.
- Efflux Corporate helps enterprises handle over 15,000 clients per month.
- Efflux Corporate is only available in an annual contract at $900 per month.
